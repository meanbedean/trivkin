About Trivkin

Every weave tells a
story of the earth

Trivkin was born from a deep reverence for nature and the ancient craft of bamboo weaving. We partner with local artisans to create lampshades that are more than light fixtures — they are pieces of living art.

Each shade starts as raw bamboo or cane, split, softened, and patiently woven by hand into forms that cast the warmest, most organic light imaginable. No two pieces are identical. That's the point.
